Zomato Redesign

I redesigned Zomato in a way where the delivery partner's convenience
is given priority over that of the customer's. This can help reduce
the discrimination and harsh treatment that delivery partners face.
This is a project I did for my assessment.

Party Popper

For my last class we were asked to code a basic interactive fiction.
It is surely one of the most challenging and exciting tasks I have
done. It is based around the reader being at a party, and at every
stage, the reader will be presented with a couple of options. The
option they choose eventually paves the path for the storyline. I
intend on improving this code soon.
