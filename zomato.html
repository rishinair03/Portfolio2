Zomato Redesign

For our Summative Assessment held in college, our class was asked to redesign any food
delivery app in a way where you make it inconvenient for the user and convenient for the
delivery partners. This is the submission I had presented. I played around with the visuals
so that the user gets confused and irritated. I also removed a few of the most convenient
features for the users such as 'saved addresses' and 'order again'. I also flipped the
rating system so that there is no unfair reviews given to the delivery partners. The
user will also be not given any information about the delivery partner apart from their
names. However, I have not made it too inconvenient for the user; or you could say it is
convenient enough for the user to return. I have maintained the design aesthetic of the
original Zomato app.
